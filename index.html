Mission Statement

Team Konoha has come together to create an accessible program for
young children to begin their journey through the landscape of their own
mental health. This will be done with our website called “ABC Emotions”
which will consist of an interactive game for children to complete, along
with a database of third party resources for parents to access as they try
and help their children through their childhood, adolescence, and even
adulthood. The intention with this project is to destigmatize mental health
discussions in public or open settings, as well as help children begin to
understand their own minds through their youth.
